Notre Histoire

Born from
Obsession

AVÉLUS was founded in the quiet ateliers of Grasse by master perfumer Céleste Ivén — a belief that fragrance is not worn, but inhabited. Every bottle holds a world built from the rarest raw materials, aged with patience, and released only when the composition achieves absolute silence.

We do not follow trends. We do not rush creation. We are architects of memory, designing invisible monuments that outlive the moment of wearing.
